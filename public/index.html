Simple 5-Step Approval Workflow

From creation to payment completion - streamlined for efficiency

1

Create Voucher

Accounts team creates payment voucher with payee details, amount, and payment mode. System automatically generates digital signature.

2

Review & Approve

Admin reviews voucher details and verifies signature integrity. Approve or reject with optional comments.

3

Payee OTP Verification

System sends SMS OTP to registered payee mobile number. Secure two-factor authentication before payment.

4

Enter OTP

Accounts staff enters OTP received by payee. System verifies code and unlocks payment completion.

5

Complete Payment

Payment is marked complete with full audit trail. Print voucher or export to PDF with digital signature.
